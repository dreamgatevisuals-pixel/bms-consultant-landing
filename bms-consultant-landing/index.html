Why Traditional Consulting Fails $1M–$3M Businesses

Big consulting firms are built for large companies. Small businesses get priced out before real implementation happens.

The traditional model

  1. Telemarketer sets an appointment
  2. Salesperson sells a 2-day business analysis
  3. Analyst finds “problem cost” using financials
  4. Consulting sold at $395–$595/hour
  5. Projects can invoice $22,000/week + travel

What happens in reality

  • $1M–$3M businesses can’t sustain the burn rate
  • Projects get cut short when invoices hit
  • Implementation stalls—owners never see results
  • Large firms won’t market below $3M because of their overhead
The opportunity: an untapped market of owners who need help, want structure, but can’t pay enterprise consulting rates.
